About Us

Smart Career Portal is a web-based platform designed to manage campus placement activities efficiently. It allows students to apply for jobs, track placement status, and stay updated on notifications.

Administrators can manage student records, companies, and placement drives with ease.

The system ensures transparency, reduces manual work, and improves coordination between students and recruiters.

Login

Admin Login: Manage students, companies, and placement drives efficiently.

Student Login: Apply for jobs, track placement status, and stay updated with notifications.

The portal provides secure access for both administrators and students with an easy-to-use interface.
